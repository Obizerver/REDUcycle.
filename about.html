About REDUcycle

Our Story

REDUcycle began with a simple idea: connect the problem of e‑waste with the opportunity to teach digital skills to underprivileged children. We operate student‑led collection drives in the UAE and channel proceeds into live, beginner‑friendly tech literacy workshops with partner schools in India.

Vision & Mission

  • Vision: A world where no child is left behind in the digital era.
  • Mission: Turn e‑waste into education through responsible recycling and targeted teaching.

Our Model

Collect

School & community drives.

Recycle

Partner ITADs & recyclers.

Fund

Proceeds tracked for transparency.

Teach

Live workshops for kids in India.

Timeline

  1. Collect

    Run donation drives; log devices responsibly.

  2. Recycle

    Data wipe, sort (A/B/C/D), and move to certified partners.

  3. Fund

    Channel proceeds to education with transparent reports.

  4. Teach

    Deliver 45‑minute live sessions using a set curriculum.

We turn e-waste in Dubai into digital literacy in India.
